About Me

I was born in Cozumel, Mexico. The most eastern island off the coast of Mexico.
At the age of 16, I moved to the United Kingdom to finish Highschool. After a gap year, I enrolled in Mechanical Engineering at University of Bristol. At university I was not certain what direction I wanted to take in my professional career, until I read about Artificial Intelligence. It was fascinating to say the least. But with zero overlap with my engineering studies, it was tough to allocate quality time and energy into it.
Since my graduation in the summer of 2020, I have dedicated every weekday to learning Machine Learning.
